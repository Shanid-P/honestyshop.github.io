Honesty Shop - At a glance

"The great aim of education is not knowledge but action."

-Herbert Spencer

Though students are often told to think deeply about course content and skills, sometimes it feels like they never progress beyond surface-level understanding. One of the best ways to help them get to deeper learning is to have them use what they have learned in a new way. With this aim in mind,the Plus Two students of NAMHSS Peringathur with the guidance of the faculty of The Department of Commerce and Management have conceived and materialized a new and unique e-shopping venture with the brand name Honesty Shop.It has now been all set to cater for the maximum conceivable needs of the targeted customers. It also aims at inculcating in the students community the 21st-century online marketing business skills which encompass entrepreneurship, communication, marketing, web designing, content creation, online transactions,data analysis and much more. It was officially inaugurated by our principal Dr. NA Muhammad Rafeeque on 3 Aug 2022.
